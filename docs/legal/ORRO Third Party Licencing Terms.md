###  ORRO Third Party Licensing Terms

This agreement governs the use of ORRO Protocol's Know Your Artist (KYA) Trust Layer by third-party platforms ("Partner").

##### 1. Legally Binding Agreement Between Two Parties

This is a binding agreement between ORRO Protocol ("ORRO") and the Partner. By integrating ORRO KYA services (API access, badge display), Partner agrees to these terms.

##### 2. Scope (Restricted But Not Limited To)

This agreement covers:

* Integration and display of ORRO Verified badges and Trust Score data via KYA API.

* Sharing of mutually beneficial, non-sensitive information about services and verified creators.

* Limitation of legal liability for trust signals.

##### 3. Partner Agrees To:

* Link badges to the official ORRO verifier ([https://orroprotocol.io/verify/](https://orroprotocol.io/verify/)).

* Use KYA data only for creator highlighting — no discrimination, no false claims (e.g., "ORRO Guaranteed").

* Not cache or store private activity data — use public proofs only.

* Notify ORRO of any detected misuse of badges within 48 hours.

* Publicly disclose the ORRO partnership in platform documentation.

* Comply with applicable laws (e.g., GDPR, CCPA) when processing KYA data.

##### 4. Partner Agrees Not To:

* Claim ORRO endorsement beyond verified reputation.

* Use KYA data for financial underwriting, lending, or custodial decisions.

* Modify, rebrand, or hide ORRO attribution on badges.

* Share KYA proofs with unauthorized third parties.

* Discriminate against creators based on Trust Score alone.

##### 5. ORRO Agrees To:

* Provide KYA API access, documentation, and support.

* Supply standard grey badge assets and optional hybrid templates (enterprise tier).

* Maintain neutral, time-proven Trust Score calculation.

* Notify Partner of major protocol changes affecting KYA output.

##### 6. ORRO Agrees Not To:

* Guarantee financial outcomes or creator reliability beyond reputation signals.

* Share Partner-specific usage data without consent.

* Favor any marketplace in scoring or syndication.

##### 7. Dispute Resolution

Disputes resolved in order:

1. Direct negotiation (14 days).

2. Mediation via neutral third party (e.g., Swiss Chambers).

3. Binding arbitration under Swiss law (neutral jurisdiction).

##### 8. Jurisdictional Concerns

This agreement is governed by Swiss law — chosen for neutrality in decentralized protocols. Both parties waive claims exceeding direct damages.

##### 9. Mandatory Advocacy

Both parties commit to:

* Promote ethical, non-financial reputation in the creative ecosystem.

* Advocate for time-proven intent over paid verification or hype.

##### 10. Ethics & Empathy

We build for real creators. Both parties agree to act in good faith, prioritizing long-term trust over short-term gain.

##### 11. Termination & Revocation

* Either party may terminate with 30 days notice.

* ORRO may immediately revoke KYA access for breach — badge displays "Revoked" publicly.

* Upon termination, Partner must cease badge display within 24 hours.

* No refunds for enterprise fees on revocation or as agreed separately for hybrid badges.
