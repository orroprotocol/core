### ORRO Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 14, 2025**

### 1. Introduction

ORRO Pty. Ltd. ("**ORRO**" or "**we**", "**our**", or "**us**") is a social media platform that lets people discuss the future and predict the value of assets created by creators within the ecosystem. Because we ask users to share ideas, forecasts, and data, protecting your privacy is core to our mission. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, the choices you have, and the rights you can exercise.

By using ORRO—whether through our website, mobile apps, or any other services we provide (collectively, the "**Services**")—you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our Services.

### 2. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to personal information processed in connection with the Services. It does **not** apply to third-party websites or services that we do not control, even if they link to, or are accessible from, ORRO. This policy applies to [orroprotocol.io](https://www.google.com/search?q=https://orroprotocol.io/ "null") and associated services.

### 3. Information We Collect

### 3.1 Information You Provide to Us

* **Account details** – display name, username, email address, password, and, if you choose, a profile picture or avatar.

* **Content** – posts, comments, predictions, market questions, signals, messages, and any other content you upload or create.

* **Play-money balances & transactions** – the virtual currency ("ORROT"), trade orders, positions, and associated financial-like activity.

* **Verification data** – documents, photos, or other information required to verify your identity for certain high-trust features (e.g., Know Your Artist, KYA).

* **Communications** – records of any messages you send to us or other users via the Services.

### 3.2 Information We Collect Automatically

* **Public Activity Data** – We automatically collect and log detailed activity data related to all actions taken on the Services (e.g., project seals, resurrection participation, governance votes, content sharing, rewards claimed). This data is the foundation for the **Know Your Artist (KYA) Trust Score** calculation and is publicly verifiable via our Hybrid Ledger for audit purposes.

* **Usage and Device Data** – This includes IP addresses, device identifiers, browser type, operating system, and information about how you interact with the Services (e.g., pages viewed, time spent on the app, click paths).

* **Cookies and Tracking** – We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to remember your preferences, analyze usage, and customize your experience. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

### 4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following primary purposes:

* To **Provide, Maintain, and Improve** the Services.

* To **Verify** your identity and authenticate your access to certain features.

* To **Calculate and Display** your **KYA Trust Score** and associated governance level.

* To **Enable Social Interactions** such as peer connections, messaging, and community discussions.

* To **Monitor Compliance** with our Terms of Service and Trust Charter, using the **Advanced Protocol Implementation System (APIS)** to prevent fraud and misuse.

* To **Communicate** with you about your account, security alerts, and service updates.

* To **Personalize** your experience and recommend relevant content.

* To **Conduct Research** and analysis to improve our protocol's performance and governance mechanisms.

### 5. Sharing of Information

We share your information in the following ways:

* **Publicly Available Information** – Your display name, profile picture, public posts, project content, and **KYA Trust Score** are publicly visible on the Services and are exposed through search engines and third-party APIs. This public data forms the basis of the decentralized reputation layer.

* **Service Providers** – We share information with third-party vendors and consultants who perform services for us (e.g., database hosting, analytics, security audits). These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements.

* **Business Partners (KYA Enterprise API)** – We share the **KYA Trust Score** and public activity proofs with third-party marketplaces and institutions through the **KYA Enterprise API** under strict licensing terms. This allows our partners to feature your verified reputation without us exposing your raw activity history.

* **Legal Compliance** – We may disclose information when required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena or other legal process, or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or the safety of our users.

* **Merger or Sale** – If ORRO is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

### 6. Your Data Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

* **Right to Access** – You can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.

* **Right to Rectification** – You can ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information.

* **Right to Erasure ("Right to Be Forgotten")** – You can request that we delete your personal information. Note that, due to the decentralized and auditable nature of the ORRO Protocol's Hybrid Ledger, certain proofs of event (`&tran_id`) cannot be deleted, but they can be anonymized or disassociated from your identity.

* **Right to Restrict Processing** – You can request that we limit the way we use your information.

* **Right to Data Portability** – You can request a transferable copy of the personal information you have provided to us.

* **Right to Object** – You can object to the processing of your personal information in certain circumstances.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the address provided in Section 15.

### 7. Global Data Protection (GDPR/CCPA)

We process personal information based on:

1. Your **Consent** (e.g., for optional marketing communications).

2. **Contractual Necessity** (e.g., to provide the Services you requested).

3. **Legitimate Interests** (e.g., for fraud prevention, security, and calculating the KYA Trust Score).

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), UK, or California, we are committed to upholding your rights under GDPR and CCPA, respectively.

### 8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as necessary to provide you with the Services. We also retain data when retention is required by law. After account deletion, we typically erase or anonymize data within 30 days, except backups and logs retained for security and compliance.

### 9. Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information, including encryption in transit, access controls, and regular security audits. No system is 100% secure; we encourage you to use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication.

### 10. International Data Transfers

ORRO is headquartered in Australia. When you use our Services, your information may be processed in, and transferred to, countries outside of your own that may not offer the same level of data protection. We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses or other legal mechanisms to safeguard EU/UK data transfers.

### 11. Children's Privacy

ORRO is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required by local law). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn a child has provided personal data, we will delete it.

### 12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are material, we will provide prominent notice (e.g., via email or an in-app banner) and, where required, obtain your consent.

The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates the effective date.
