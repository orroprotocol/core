# ORRO The Golden Ratio

## The ORRO Golden Ratio: 1 Fragment = 1 Trust Score

The trust ecosystem is now governed by a single, revolutionary metric that emphasizes endurance and integrity over social complexity.

* **1:1 Ratio**: For every single fragment a user mints to the ledger, they gain exactly one point in their **Trust Score**.

* **Uncapped Potential**: The previous 1,000-point limit has been removed. Total accumulation is now unlimited, allowing a lifetime of "Grit" to be accurately represented as a verifiable reputation metric.

* **Proof of Integrity**: Because fragments are anchored in time and cannot be moved, a high Trust Score serves as undeniable proof of a creator's consistency and long-term commitment to the "Human Lead".

## Revised Tiered Leveling Logic

Leveling is now strictly tied to the **Trust Score**, providing a clear roadmap for community progression. Based on the BETA "Daily-Only" requirement, the milestones are as follows:

| **Level** | **Rank**         | **Trust Score Requirement** | **Estimated Time Commitment** |
| --------- | ---------------- | --------------------------- | ----------------------------- |
| **1**     | **Supporter**    | 100 Points                  | ~3 Months (Daily)             |
| **2**     | **Participator** | 200 Points                  | ~6 Months (Daily)             |
| **3**     | **Contributor**  | 300 Points                  | ~9 Months (Daily)             |
| **4**     | **Creator**      | 400 Points                  | ~12 Months (Daily)            |

## Operational Impact on UI and Governance

1. **Menu Access (UI V3.0 Sync)**: The software now uses the Trust Score as the primary gatekeeper for functionality. For example, the **Projects** menu (formerly Level 1) now unlocks automatically once a user reaches a Trust Score of **400**.

2. **Gossip Box Visibility**: High Trust Scores grant users "respect and admiration" within the global feed, as their rank is a direct reflection of their cumulative creative history.

3. **Fragment Zero (Genesis)**: Every journey begins with **Fragment 0**, which initializes the user's first Trust Point and anchors their identity to the protocol.

This simplification ensures that the protocol remains "Human Lead" by rewarding the simple, honest act of showing up every day.

## Technical Efficiency & Cost Reduction

* **Reduced Computational Overhead:** The protocol no longer needs to calculate weighted averages for winks, favourites, or community interactions to determine a rank. Validation is now a simple "count" operation on the ledger.

* **Lower Gas Fees:** In the **Advanced Fragment Creation** tier, "Unlimited Cycles" are restricted only by protocol gas fees. By simplifying the trust logic, the data payload per fragment is minimized, making it significantly cheaper for creators to maintain long-term, high-volume projects.

* **Instant Level Validation:** Leveling up (from Supporter to Creator) no longer requires a complex audit. The system simply checks if `FragmentCount >= 400` to instantly unlock the **Left Hand Menu**'s "Projects" and "Advanced" features.

## The "Endurance" Identity

* **Objective Integrity:** In this new system, trust cannot be "gamed" through social manipulation. Because fragments are **anchored in time** and cannot be moved, a Trust Score of 400 is a mathematical proof of a 12-month "Human Lead".

* **Respect Through Grit:** As you noted, removing the 1,000-point cap allows users to accumulate unlimited points over their lifetime. This transforms the **Right Hand Menu**'s "Trusts" and "Profile" sections into a display of pure creative endurance that earns genuine admiration from the community.

## BETA Streamlining

* **The Daily Grind:** Limiting the BETA to **Daily fragments** creates a high-velocity data stream that is incredibly easy to validate.

* **Fragment Zero (Genesis):** This single variable starts at the very first moment (Fragment 0), ensuring the chain of trust is unbroken from the second a user joins the protocol.
