# ORRO Gossip Box Rules

## Temporary Ban

These bans are applied automatically by the protocol's monitoring layer. Access is restored only after the lockout period expires.

* **Off-Topic Gossiping:** Discussing subjects unrelated to the creative mission of ORRO.

* **Repeating Gossip:** Spamming the same information or flooding the feed.

* **Frequent/Excessive Gossiping:** High-volume chatter that creates "noise" rather than value.

## Permanent Ban

The following violations result in a permanent revocation of your ability to post to the Gossip Box.

* **Malicious Nature:** Any intent to harm or deceive the community.

* **Sexual or Deviant Content:** Posting explicit or inappropriate material.

* **Hate Speech & Extremism:** Promoting hate speech or controversial religious/political agendas.

* **Harassment & Bullying:** Targeted aggression or intimidation of any user.
