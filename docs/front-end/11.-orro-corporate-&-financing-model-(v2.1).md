## 2. Token Utility & Tiered Assets

To ensure long-term stability and investor protection, ORRO utilizes a three-token ecosystem:

| **Asset**          | **Ticker**  | **Purpose**                                         | **Targeted Ratio** | **Interest**      |
| ------------------ | ----------- | --------------------------------------------------- | ------------------ | ----------------- |
| **ORRO Community** | **\$ORROC** | Reward token for ecosystem contributions.           | 10:1 USD           | 2.5–5% (Variable) |
| **ORRO Tether**    | **\$ORROT** | Main utility token for all platform tasks.          | 100:1 USD          | 1–1.5% (Variable) |
| **ORRO Stock**     | **\$ORROS** | Equity shares. Minting 1 share burns 1,000 \$ORROT. | 1000:1 USD         | 5–10% (Variable)  |

## 3. Investor Safety: The "Trust" Buy-Back Scheme

ORRO stands behind its early supporters with a guaranteed exit floor during the transition from Alpha to BETA.

* **Alpha Service Buy-Back:** For every 10 tokens spent on platform services (e.g., Trust Mode), ORRO will buy back those tokens at fair market value plus a **2.5% "Goodwill" bonus**.

* **ICO Protection Guarantee:** Following the completion of Phase 2, users may request a full reimbursement for up to **50% of their total ICO purchase**.
  
  * **Requirement 1:** Tokens must remain in the original wallet (no transfers or sales).
  
  * **Requirement 2:** Successful completion of a Wallet Audit and Identity Check.

### 4. Operational Sustainability

The **ORRO Community Fund (ORROC)** is established to protect the protocol's infrastructure. 5% of the total 1 Billion supply is reserved for:

> * **Infrastructure:** Server costs and high-frequency ledger maintenance (25%).
> * **Expert Engineering:** Senior Developer standby fund for critical bug fixes (25%).
> * **Ecosystem Growth:** Weekly "Grit" competitions and creator giveaways (25%).
> * **Risk Mitigation:** General contingency fund (25%).
