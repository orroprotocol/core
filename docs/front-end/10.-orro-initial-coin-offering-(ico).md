To ensure ecosystem stability, participants must choose a single path: either **Liquidity Exit** (Offer 1) or **Equity Transition** (Offer 2). Opting into one program explicitly excludes the participant from the other.

## 5. Share Transferability & Secondary Market Liquidity

While ORRO shares are not initially intended for public listing on centralized equity exchanges (CEX), the protocol is designed to support organic liquidity and peer-to-peer (P2P) movement.

* **Transferability Status:** All shares issued under the **Equity Transition** are fully transferable between verified participants. Shares are held in private registries and can be moved off-market via standard legal assignment or digital certificate transfer.

* **Secondary Market Access:** * **Community Liquidity Hub:** A dedicated "Buy & Sell" marketplace will be established within the **ORRO Community Threads**. This provides a transparent environment for members to negotiate bilateral trades directly.
  
  * **Decentralized Marketplaces (DEX):** The protocol may facilitate the creation of a **DEX liquidity pool** (e.g., on Solana/Raydium) specifically for "wrapped" share certificates. This would allow for 24/7 automated market-making (AMM) outside of traditional trading hours.

* **Listing Contingency:** Trading remains restricted to private and P2P environments unless the board approves a formal transition to a public exchange. Until such an event, liquidity is driven by community demand and the internal "Triple-Token Flywheel" mechanics.
